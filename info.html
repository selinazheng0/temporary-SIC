Shanzhai is culture aims to break stigmas around the bootleg practices that take place in China. It encourages shanzhai practice as a tool to inform graphic design and design practices at large. This site is the digital sister resource to the mircopublication.

Shanzhai is inherently cultural to China. The term originates from traditional Chinese folktale, "Water Margin." The story follows 108 heroes rebelling against the oppressive empire. Shanzhai meaning "mountain stronghold" was the name of their base. This rebellious ethos is still relevant today, especially within the context of design.

The Gallery is an interactive user-upload platform featuring found and original creations of shanzhai imagery.

The Reference page is a library dedicated to texts that are related to shanzhai. It contains all sources referenced in the zine publication and many more relevant texts to inform your very own shanzhai practice.

Shanzhai Seminar

The Shanzhai Seminar was an in-person event that took place on March 12, 2026. The event consisted of an interactive lecture, discussing questions related to bootlegging and introduction to various manifestations of shanzhai practice. The event concluded with a shanzhai making activity inspired by the research subject of Shanzhai Lyric.

A recording of the presentation and group discussion was captured for those unable to attend the event.
